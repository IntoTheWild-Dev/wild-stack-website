Product 02
Figma Community Plugin

ITW CMYK Plugin — Print Ready

Professional print production, directly inside Figma

The Problem

Figma is a screen-first design tool — and it shows. When designers hand off print work, they hit the same wall every time: no CMYK support, no bleed settings, no crop marks, and no way to know if their images will hold up at print resolution. Costly back-and-forth with print bureaus, last-minute fixes in Illustrator or InDesign, and workflows that shouldn't be broken in the first place.

Our Solution

Print Ready is a Figma plugin that brings professional print production directly into the design environment. No extra software. No file conversion. No surprises at the printer.

Features

01
Page Setup
Industry-standard presets (A4, US Letter, Business Card, and more) or define custom sizes.
02
Bleed & Crop Marks
Configure bleed and add crop and registration marks — all rendered as vector paths.
03
DPI Audit
Check image resolution before export with a colour-coded quality report.
04
PDF Export
Export PDFs in CMYK or RGB with embedded ICC profiles (FOGRA39, GRACoL, and more).
